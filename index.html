Pentatonix (Deluxe Edition)

A review website

Released October 16, 2015, "Pentatonix (Deluxe Edition)" is the a cappella group's first original album. In just a few days, it's already climbing the charts rapidly, now #1 on iTunes and as of October 26, 2015, #1 on Billboard.

Pentatonix is a five person a cappella group from Arlington, Texas whose beginnings started by winning The Sing-Off in 2011. Their main style is pop, and their music is well known for it's tight harmonies, vocal riffs, strong bass lines, unique vocal percussion, and sick drops.
